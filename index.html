Why a mouse for a logo? Often it’s the first question that I get. I suppose I could have just put my name in bold and have been done with it. But a name is simply who I am, not what I am.


What am I? Well a lot of things.


For one I am versatile. From graphic design, illustration, social media, web development and even motion graphics. I’ve worked in an ever expanding series of roles. A mouse is nimble and adaptive and like the mouse I’ve managed to fit myself into niches that we didn’t even know we needed until we did.


Much like a mouse I’m also smart. I kinda have to be, I’m not the biggest thing out there and I know that. I’ve worked with clients and personalities both large and small and have always delivered. Dates change and deadlines may shift but when your clever no task is beyond reach.


Lastly, I can be heard. How? Mice by nature are rather quiet but you always know when one is around. Why? Because everyone tells you. My work has a life of it’s own and with enough people talking the story tells itself. A mouse’s presence is proof that you needn’t be loud to make some noise.
